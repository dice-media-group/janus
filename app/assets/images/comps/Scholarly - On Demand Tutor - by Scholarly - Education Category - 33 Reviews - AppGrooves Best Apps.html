App Description



	

				

				
Hire a reputable tutor nearby within minutes with Scholarly— no more long bookings, or expensive fees.
From
 low-cost to premium, K-12 to Graduate, Scholarly has got you covered 
with professional tutors in your area. Search for a local tutor on the 
go anytime, anywhere with Scholarly. Made by students, for students, 
Scholarly will help you succeed in your classes, tests, or thirst for 
knowledge. 

Get help for homework or tests in any subject from 
Math, Science, English, Computer Sciences, Physics, MSAT, LSAT, SAT 
Prep, and much more!

Scholarly is an on demand tutoring service 
that connects clients to tutors in real time. We strive to create the 
best experience possible for our clients, as well as foster a 
professional community of tutors. 

MAKE GREAT MONEY WITH SCHOLARLY
Tutor on your own terms. Meet friendly people in your city and help them learn—no office or boss.
Make Your Own Schedule. Tutor full-time or just a few hours a week, whenever it’s convenient for you.
Use Your Own Phone. It’s free to download on your own Android device.


HOW DOES THE SCHOLARLY APP WORK?
We'll automatically build your profile for you, to get you started quickly. 
Receive offers from clients anytime


HOW DO I GET STARTED?
Download the Scholarly app from www.scholarly.us
Sign up to tutor by selecting the Tutor checkbox during the sign up page.
Attach your resume, and email to hello@scholarly.us
Once you are registered and approved, tutor and earn money whenever you want.

MORE INFORMATION
visit us at http://www.scholarly.us

Contact us here for any question: hello@scholarly.us
